About

Changing the publishing game.

Advertising is a broken model and reader supported content allows for diversity and sustainability. Proxima is created to bring the model of subscriber supported newsletters and publishing to emerging markets.

15 years ago La Diaria was founded on the idea that a cooperative of journalists could come together and create a daily newspaper funded by subscribers and delivered directly to homes instead of being sold through a corrupt system of kiosks.

Proxima is a new project to democratize sustainable journalism in emerging markets. Using the substack model of paid newsletters we're providing local in market payments, a sophisticated website cms, publishing to WhatsApp, a system of cross promotion for authors, and a fellowship to fund new newsletter businesses.

We're adapting the subscription content model to latin america and emerging markets. Our team has decades of experience in creating new digital platforms and sustainable media companies in the region. We've got a strong network of journalists and we're going to grow together.

Creator ownership. Part of a larger community.

01

Your Subscribers

Creators on proxima retain all ownership of their content and the relationship with their subscribers. You can leave any time

02

Shared Ownership

A portion of Proxima's stock is owned by a coop which creators on the platform can join. You create proxima and you can participate as an owner.

03

Cross Promotion & Discovery

You're not alone, you can help your fellow authors by including related cross promotion of content and newsletters. This allows everybody to grow together.
