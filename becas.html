Supporting Writers.

Proxima has limited funds to support writers migrating to a reader supported newsletter.

Funds to get started. A newsletter is launching a new business. For select journalists we're offering a fellowship.

Fellowship to cover minimal expenses at the start

Proxima has a fellowship fund to cover the first six months of minimal expenses while a promising journalist builds up their audience. This is available to journalists who show particular promise and need help getting started. The fellowship is regenerative and once a newsletter generates income then a portion of that revenue goes back to provide funds for future authors.

Monthly Stipends. For promising journalists to cover startup expenses.

Apply for a proxima fellowship and we'll cover minimal living expenses while you build up an audience which can support your newsletter.

The fellowship isn't a loan, simply funding to get started. If your newsletter fails then you don't have pay back the stipend.

Support the fellowship. Successful newsletters contribute back.

Established fellows will contribute back in to a common fund as a small percentage of monthly revenue to a common fund. This money is used to support new fellows.
